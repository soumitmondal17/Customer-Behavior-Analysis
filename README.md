# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project explores customer shopping behavior using transactional data from 3,900 purchases across multiple product categories.  
The objective is to uncover insights into spending habits, customer segments, product preferences, and subscription patterns to support data‑driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
A retail company wants to better understand its customers to improve:
- Sales performance
- Customer satisfaction
- Long‑term loyalty

**Core Question:**  
How can customer shopping data be leveraged to identify trends, improve engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

---

## 🚀 Deliverables
1. **Data Preparation (Python)**  
   - Clean and transform raw datasets  
   - Handle missing values, duplicates, and formatting issues  

2. **Data Analysis (SQL)**  
   - Structure data into tables  
   - Run queries to extract insights on customer segments, loyalty, and purchase drivers  

3. **Visualization (Power BI)**  
   - Build interactive dashboards  
   - Highlight key trends and patterns for stakeholders  

4. **Report & Presentation**  
   - Summarize findings and recommendations  
   - Create a presentation with clear visuals and actionable insights  


---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python** (Pandas)  
- **SQL** (PostgreSQL)  
- **Power BI** (Interactive dashboards)  
- **GitHub** (Version control & collaboration)  

---

## 📈 Expected Outcomes
- Identify top product categories and purchase drivers  
- Understand repeat buyer behavior and subscription likelihood  
- Provide actionable recommendations for marketing and product strategy
